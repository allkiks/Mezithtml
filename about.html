Brief Background

MeziT is an offshoot of an Advanced Metering Project initiated at Standard Chartered Ventures in 2019 in collaboration with Safaricom and later joined by Float IoT Netherlands.


A proof-of-concept project was completed at Eldowas, where the application of IoT advanced metering and the efficacy of narrowband GSM IoT telemetry was validated.


Commercial losses of 46% were identified at KenyaRe district metered zone.


In 2022 incorporation process commenced, and the venture was independently registered as MeziT in 2023.

Recent Milestones

Partnership with Ingeniousware of Germany for their AI-driven hydraulic modeling for large capabilities.

Our Approach

At MeziT, we believe in taking a comprehensive approach to tackle water loss and ensure financial sustainability.


To achieve this goal, we bring together all key stakeholders in the water industry, including government bodies, water utility companies, regulators, and communities.


By fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing, we create an ecosystem that drives innovation, efficiency, and sustainable water management practices

Partnerships and Collaborations

We establish strong partnerships with government agencies, water utility companies, regulatory bodies, and research institutions.


By collaborating with these stakeholders, we create an ecosystem for sharing knowledge, best practices, and policy frameworks.


Together, we aim to overcome challenges, accelerate technology adoption, and drive positive change in the water sector.
